Vegetarian Pizza in Poland

Wow! This pizza was eaten during my trip to Poland in July 2019. It's incredible how long ago that was already. My friend Roy and I did not have much time to buy a meal, yet we decided to sit down and eat at this obscure restaurant in some random mall. I was amazed at how water came in beer bottles and surprised at how good this pizza was. I think I finished it.

Would definitely recommend. 10/10
