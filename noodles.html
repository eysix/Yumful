Instant Noodles Are A Lifesaver

Yup, instant noodles! Duh, everyone's favourite food. They can be a life-saver for broke, hungry college students. However, they are absolutely unhealthy, bad for our digestive systems, and really boring. This recipe has some advice and tips to make your instant noodle experience better.

Inspired by Epicurious.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : noodles Dinner food

ingredients

2 1/2 cups of water

1 pack of instant ramen

1 egg

instructions

step 1

Boil water in a kettle or saucepan over high heat. Add the soup base and vegetable mix. Boil for 1 minute.

step 2

Drop the noodles in to the broth - GENTLY. Do not break up the noodles, instead, keep noodles submerged with chopsticks or a fork.

step 3

At the two minute mark, pull the noodles from the broth and fan them for two minutes. The flow of air will stunt the cooking process, which gives the noodles a slightly more al dente texture.

step 4

Return the now-cooled noodles to the broth and add your toppings, such as an egg. Crack a raw egg over the noodles, cover the pot, and boil for 30 seconds.

step 5

Turn off the heat, cover and let sit for 30 seconds more. Serve immediately.
